Oracle - CRM

Oracle CRM, also known as Oracle Customer Relationship Management, is a comprehensive suite of customer-centric applications and solutions offered by Oracle Corporation. It provides organizations with the tools and capabilities to effectively manage customer interactions, enhance sales performance, and improve customer satisfaction.

  • Customer data integration
  • Quote and order capture
  • Partner relationship management
  • Business Intelligence (BI) applications
  • Price Management
  • CRM gadgets
  • Self-service and eBilling

FocusR CRM is a highly scalable and adaptable solution for various industries and business sizes. It enables organizations to enhance customer relationships, boost sales effectiveness, and drive customer satisfaction. With its extensive feature set, integration capabilities, and advanced analytics tools, FocusR CRM empowers businesses to optimize their customer-centric processes and achieve their sales and service objectives.
